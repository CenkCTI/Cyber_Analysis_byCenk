Strategic Analyst

A strategic analyst is at the heart of the intelligence process, managing information collection and evaluation. Analysts synthesize data to identify the strengths, weaknesses, goals, and intentions of competitors, allies, and other actors, providing actionable insights and future-oriented forecasts.

Key competencies:

Strong understanding of state objectives
Broad cultural and historical knowledge
Ability to integrate diverse areas of expertise
Imagination and creativity
Critical and analytical thinking
Effective communication, observation, and inquiry skills
Patience, composure, and a strong sense of responsibility
Proficiency in technology and data literacy

Strategic analysts anticipate the future, assess risks and threats, and support policy-making processes.
